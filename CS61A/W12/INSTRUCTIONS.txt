INSTRUCTIONS FOR CS61A WEEK 12
==================================================

1. Add your lecture materials to this directory:
   - PDF files (lecture slides, notes, handouts)
   - PowerPoint files (.ppt, .pptx)
   - Word documents (.doc, .docx)
   - Any other relevant materials

2. Name your files clearly:
   - lecture_1.pdf
   - slides_1.pdf
   - notes_1.pdf
   - etc.

3. After adding materials, run:
   python lecture_pdf_extraction.py CS61A

4. This will generate study guides in the {course_code}_New/ directory

5. For frontend integration, create:
   nextjs-frontend/src/app/api/cs61a/content/[week]/route.ts
